Slaying a Vampyre`
Go to Draynor Manor which is just north of Draynor Village.`
Enter the manor and go to the east side.`
Walk down the stairs.`
Open the coffin in the back of the room.`
Fight Count Draynor until he is almost dead, your character will then automatically use the stake on him to kill him.`
Finishing up`
Return to Morgan.`
Quest complete!
